# Creative Brief: UGC Ad Campaign for Reminderr

This document outlines the strategic parameters for the Reminderr User-Generated Content (UGC) ad campaign. It serves as a guide for creators, designers, and media buyers to ensure consistency and conversion focus across all assets.

---

## 1. Campaign Objective
The primary objective of this campaign is to **drive app sign-ups** by showcasing how easily Reminderr integrates into a user's daily workflow. The campaign will highlight the ease of setting reminders using natural conversation and the relief of letting go of mental clutter.

---

## 2. Core Campaign Message
> **"Clear your mind, let Reminderr hold the details."**

The campaign focuses on the transition from **stress and cognitive overload** (trying to keep everything in your head) to **calm and control** (typing a single sentence and letting the AI handle it). It emphasizes that being organized shouldn't feel like a chore.

---

## 3. Brand Voice & Tone
To build trust and authenticity, the content must avoid robotic, hyper-enthusiastic "pitchy" tones.
*   **Natural:** Conversational vocabulary, pauses, and reactions.
*   **Relatable:** Acknowledging that keeping up with tasks is hard and that traditional productivity tools are often annoying.
*   **Helpful & Simple:** Explaining the app clearly without using technical jargon or buzzwords.
*   **Forbidden Words:** *Revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, unlock your potential, transform your life, synergy, next-gen.*

---

## 4. Target Audience & Pain Points

| Audience Segment | Primary Pain Points | Angle in Campaign |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Students & College Students** | Exam anxiety, missing homework deadlines, balancing study with part-time jobs. | "Set-and-forget" homework reminders in under 3 seconds. |
| **Professionals** | Overwhelming email lists, forgetting follow-ups, back-to-back meetings. | Seamless desk workflow integrations; conversational follow-up reminders. |
| **Freelancers** | Multi-client invoicing deadlines, project scope creep, wearing all business hats. | Tracking client deliverables and admin tasks without cluttering calendars. |
| **Startup Teams** | Disorganized chats, missed team deliverables, tool fatigue. | Conversational sprint-task coordination and status checks. |

---

## 5. Marketing Goals
*   **App Sign-ups:** Increase sign-ups by 25% over the 30-day campaign run.
*   **Creative Testing:** Identify which hook category (Question vs. Problem vs. Curiosity) and audience variation has the lowest Cost Per Acquisition (CPA).
*   **Engagement & Retention:** Benchmark a 3-second hook rate of >35% and a click-through rate (CTR) of >2.5% on video ads.

---

## 6. Platforms & Formats
The campaign will run as native vertical video ads across:
*   **Instagram Reels:** Focusing on aesthetic workspace layouts and quick lifestyle transitions.
*   **TikTok:** Conversational, trend-aware, text-on-screen heavy, and raw.
*   **YouTube Shorts:** Direct problem-to-solution structures with high visual clarity.
*   **Facebook Reels:** Pragmatic, lifestyle-oriented, and focused on work-life balance.

---

## 7. Campaign Deliverables
1.  **Hooks File (`hooks.md`):** 20 scroll-stopping video openers.
2.  **UGC Ad Scripts (`ugc_scripts.md`):** 10 short-form video scripts (30-60s) with 4 audience-focused variations.
3.  **Storyboards (`storyboards.md`):** Visual scene-by-scene plans for all 10 scripts.
4.  **Short-Form Ads (`short_ads.md`):** 40 platform-optimized variants (10 per platform).
5.  **Captions & CTAs (`captions.md`, `cta.md`):** High-converting social copy.
6.  **KPI Tracking Sheet (`campaign_metrics.md`):** Sheet to evaluate ad performance.
