# Campaign Metrics: Marketing KPI Sheet

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) and target metrics for the Reminderr UGC Ad Campaign. By establishing these benchmarks, the performance marketing team can evaluate which creatives and platforms are delivering the highest return on ad spend (ROAS).

---

## 1. Campaign Strategy & Goals

*   **Campaign Goal:** Drive **15,000 new app sign-ups** within a 30-day testing window.
*   **Total Test Budget:** $15,000 USD (Target Cost Per Acquisition / CPA: $1.00 per free sign-up).
*   **Target Audience Size:** ~1.2 Million across target geos (US, UK, CA, AU) focusing on:
    *   College Students (Ages 18–24)
    *   Young Professionals & Freelancers (Ages 22–35)
    *   Startup founders and team leads (Ages 25–40)

---

## 2. Global Campaign Benchmarks

| Metric | Target Benchmark | Minimum Acceptable | Definition / Calculation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hook Rate (3-Sec View)** | **35.0%** | 25.0% | Percentage of viewers who watch the first 3 seconds. |
| **Average Watch Time** | **12.0 seconds** | 8.0 seconds | Total watch time divided by total video plays. |
| **Retention Rate (15-Sec)** | **15.0%** | 10.0% | Percentage of viewers remaining at the 15-second mark. |
| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | **2.50%** | 1.80% | Outbound link clicks divided by total ad impressions. |
| **Engagement Rate** | **4.0%** | 2.5% | (Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves) / Total Views. |
| **Conversion Rate (CVR)** | **12.0%** | 8.0% | Percentage of landing page visitors who sign up for Reminderr. |

---

## 3. Platform-Specific KPI Targets

Different platforms exhibit distinct user behaviors. The table below outlines our platform-specific KPI expectations for the vertical video placements:

| Platform | Target CTR | Target CVR (Page) | Primary Success Indicator | Primary Content Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram Reels** | 2.2% | 10.0% | High Saves / Shares | High visual aesthetic, workspace setups. |
| **TikTok** | 3.1% | 14.0% | Retention Rate (15s) | Raw, high-energy, humor-driven, trend-aware. |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 2.4% | 11.0% | Average Watch Time | Step-by-step value, clear text overlays. |
| **Facebook Reels** | 1.9% | 9.0% | Link Clicks (CTR) | Practical problem-solution, office productivity. |

---

## 4. Optimization Framework

If an ad creative underperforms against these KPIs, the following actions will be taken:

1.  **Low Hook Rate (<25%):** Swap the first 3 seconds of the video using a hook from `hooks.md`. Do not re-record the whole video.
2.  **Low Retention Rate (<10%):** Re-edit the video body to speed up pacing, add faster cuts, or include more prominent text-on-screen annotations.
3.  **Low CTR (<1.8%):** Adjust the ad caption or swap the Call to Action (CTA) overlay using options from `cta.md`.
4.  **Low Conversion Rate (<8%):** Optimize the landing page sign-up flow, reduce registration fields, and ensure the landing page headline matches the ad's hooks.
