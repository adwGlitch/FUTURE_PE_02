# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [1.0.0] - 2026-07-12

### Added
- **Product Overview (`product_overview.md`)**: Comprehensive description of Reminderr, problem solved, target audience, USP, and brand positioning.
- **Creative Brief (`creative_brief.md`)**: Core strategy documentation aligning campaign goals, tone of voice, platforms, and deliverables.
- **Scroll-Stopping Hooks (`hooks.md`)**: 20 conversion-focused hooks categorized into Questions, Problems, Curiosity, Emotional, and Statistics.
- **UGC Scripts & Audience Variations (`ugc_scripts.md`)**: 10 distinct 30-60 second scripts with detailed story hooks, demonstrations, and CTAs, alongside specific tone-tailored versions for Students, Professionals, Freelancers, and Startup Teams.
- **Storyboards (`storyboards.md`)**: Scene-by-scene breakdown (visuals, camera angles, captions) for all 10 UGC scripts.
- **Short Form Ads (`short_ads.md`)**: 40 platform-optimized scripts (10 Instagram Reels, 10 YouTube Shorts, 10 Facebook Reels, and 10 TikTok Shorts).
- **Captions (`captions.md`)**: 15 platform-ready caption variations with strategic emoji placements and CTAs.
- **Call to Actions (`cta.md`)**: 20 distinct conversion variations focusing on app sign-ups.
- **Campaign Metrics (`campaign_metrics.md`)**: Performance targets and KPI sheet for measuring CTR, engagement, and conversion rates.
- **Prompt Engineering & Evaluation (`documentation.md`, `evaluation.md`, `prompts.md`)**: Full workflow records from V1 to V2 to Final Prompt, analysis of performance, and prompt design techniques.
- **Checklist & LinkedIn Post (`linkedin_and_checklist.md`)**: Student-toned announcement copy and project verification checklist.
- **Project Structure**: Organized file layout matching task specifications including `assets/` and `outputs/` folders.
