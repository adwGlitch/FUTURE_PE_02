# Prompt Database: Reminderr UGC Campaign

This file contains the exact prompts used throughout the iterative development of the Reminderr UGC Ad Content Pack.

---

## 1. Prompt V1: Initial Trial
*   **Purpose:** Simple prompt to generate basic scripts.
*   **Type:** Basic instruction.

```text
Write 10 UGC ad scripts for a reminder and productivity app called Reminderr. The app is for students, freelancers, and professionals to track deadlines and stay organized. The scripts should be about 30 to 60 seconds long.
```

---

## 2. Prompt V2: Persona & Negative Constraints
*   **Purpose:** Introduce a specialized persona, structural constraints, and a list of banned corporate clichés.
*   **Type:** Role-based prompting with negative constraints.

```text
You are an expert Performance Marketer and UGC Content Creator. Write 10 short-form UGC ad scripts (30-60 seconds) for Reminderr, an AI-powered reminder and productivity web application.

Target Audience: Students, College Students, Professionals, Freelancers, Teams.
Brand Personality: Modern, Friendly, Professional, Trustworthy, Simple.
Tone: Authentic, Conversational, Relatable, Natural, Human.

Each script must follow this structure:
1. Hook (Scroll-stopper in first 3 seconds)
2. Problem (Mental clutter, missed deadlines, app fatigue)
3. Personal Experience (How the creator struggled)
4. Solution (Introduction of Reminderr)
5. Product Demo (What is shown on screen)
6. Call to Action (CTA to sign up/try app)

CONSTRAINTS:
- Do NOT use typical robotic AI words: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "unlock your potential", "transform your life".
- Write the dialogue exactly as a real person would speak (use casual transitions like "honestly", "literally", "so").
- Do not make the scripts sound like a sales pitch. It must sound like a real user sharing an experience.
```

---

## 3. Final Prompt: Production-Ready UGC Content Pack
*   **Purpose:** Complete system instructions to generate the entire campaign package, including 20 hooks, 10 scripts with detailed 4-scene storyboards, 40 short-form variants, 15 captions, 20 CTAs, and campaign metrics.
*   **Type:** Structured template prompting with multi-perspective instructions.

```text
You are an expert Prompt Engineer, Performance Marketing Strategist, UGC Creative Director, and Social Media Advertiser.

Your task is to create a complete, professional, portfolio-ready UGC Ad Content Pack for a productivity platform called "Reminderr". 

Product Context:
Reminderr is an AI-powered productivity app that helps users organize their daily lives through natural language reminder scheduling, task management, and context-aware nudges.

Brand Guidelines:
- Tone: Natural, Conversational, Relatable, Human.
- Forbidden Words: revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, next-gen, unlock your potential, transform your life.

Generate the following components:
1. Product Overview: Describe the product, problem solved, target audience, USP, and brand positioning.
2. Creative Brief: Campaign objective, core message, brand voice, target audience, pain points, marketing goals, platforms, and deliverables.
3. Hooks: 20 scroll-stopping hooks categorized into Question, Problem, Curiosity, Emotional, and Statistic hooks.
4. UGC Scripts: 10 scripts (30-60s) with Hook, Problem, Personal Story, Solution, Product Demo description, and CTA. Tone must sound like a real user.
5. Audience Variations: Student, Professional, Freelancer, and Startup Team variations.
6. Storyboards: For every UGC script, create a 4-scene storyboard showing Camera Angle, Suggested Visuals, and Suggested Captions/VO.
7. Short-Form Ads: 10 Instagram Reels, 10 YouTube Shorts, 10 Facebook Reels, and 10 TikTok Shorts.
8. Captions: 15 natural captions with natural emojis and ending in a CTA.
9. Call to Actions: 20 CTA variations.
10. Campaign Metrics: A marketing KPI sheet showing targets for CTR, Watch Time, Retention, Engagement, and CVR.

Output Formatting:
Format everything in clean Markdown. Use headings, lists, blockquotes, and tables for storyboards and metrics to make the final output look like a professional agency deliverable.
```
